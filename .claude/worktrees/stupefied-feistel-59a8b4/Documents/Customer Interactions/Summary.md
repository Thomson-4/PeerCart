# 🗣️ Customer Interview Summary – PeerCart

## 📌 Overview:
We conducted direct interactions with multiple users, including students and young professionals, to understand real-world challenges in buying, selling, and renting items.

---

## 🔍 Key Findings:

- Most users expressed **concerns about trust and safety**, especially due to fake users and scams on existing platforms.

- Many users rely on **informal methods** such as WhatsApp groups, friends, or social media, which are often unorganized and inefficient.

- Users reported that finding the right buyer or seller is **time-consuming and frustrating**.

- A majority of users showed interest in a **verified platform** where users are authenticated.

- The concept of **renting items** (instead of buying) was highly appreciated, especially for short-term needs.

- Users liked the idea of **posting their requirements** and letting sellers reach out, instead of searching manually.

- Location-based (nearby) matching was seen as a **very useful feature** for faster transactions.

---

## 💡 Insights Gained:

- Trust is the **biggest pain point** in current marketplaces.
- Users prefer **simple, fast, and reliable solutions**.
- There is a strong demand for a **structured and dedicated platform**.

---

## 🎯 Conclusion:

Customer interactions clearly validated the need for a platform like **PeerCart**.  
The feedback confirmed that features such as **user verification, renting, smart matching, and requirement posting** directly address real user problems and can significantly improve the overall experience.
