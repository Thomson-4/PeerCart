# 🚀 PeerCart – Smart Peer-to-Peer Marketplace

## 📌 Overview
PeerCart is a smart, AI-powered peer-to-peer marketplace that enables users to **buy, sell, and rent items safely and efficiently** within a trusted environment.  
The platform focuses on solving real-world problems such as lack of trust, unorganized platforms, and inefficient transactions.

---

## 🧠 Problem Statement
Users face difficulty in buying and selling second-hand items due to **lack of trust, fake users, and unorganized platforms**.  
Finding reliable buyers or sellers is **time-consuming and inefficient** in existing systems.

---

## 💡 Solution
PeerCart provides a **verified, intelligent marketplace** where users can:
- Buy, sell, and rent items  
- Connect with **trusted users**  
- Complete transactions **quickly and safely**  

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Build a **safe and reliable platform** for peer-to-peer transactions  
- Reduce time and effort in finding buyers/sellers  
- Enable **renting for short-term needs**  
- Promote **sustainable usage of resources**  

---

## 👥 Target Users
- Students  
- Young professionals  
- Hostel/PG residents  
- Budget-conscious individuals  

---

## 🔑 Key Features

### 🛒 Buy • Sell • Rent
Users can list items, purchase products, or rent items for short-term use.

### 🔐 User Verification System
- Email/phone-based login  
- Verified users for safe transactions  

### ⭐ Ratings & Trust Score
- Users can rate each other  
- Builds credibility and trust  

### 🤖 AI-Based Features
- Price suggestions  
- Smart search and listing assistance  

### 📍 Nearby Matching
- Connects users within the same locality  
- Faster and more convenient transactions  

### 📢 “Post Your Need” Feature
- Users can post requirements  
- Sellers can directly respond  

---

## ⚔️ Unique Selling Proposition (USP)
- Trust-first marketplace with verification  
- Combination of **buy, sell, and rent** in one platform  
- Smart matching between buyers and sellers  
- Focus on **local and fast transactions**
